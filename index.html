Onda Mecânica provides professional audio solutions, ensuring exceptional sound quality. Our expertise ranges from sound system design and live sound for large-scale events to audio production, post-production, and acoustic projects. Engineering in the service of art, we combine technical precision with creative excellence to meet the unique requirements of each project.

Services

Live sound
In collaboration with clients and partners, Onda Mecânica handles the entire system design, setup, calibration, and operation during events. Each stage can be carried out separately based on project needs, though we prioritize full-project execution.

We provide technical direction services, involves overseeing all critical aspects of a project’s technical execution. This includes assembling specialized teams, optimizing sound systems, conducting regular maintenance, and recommending additional equipment, while managing the full logistics from start to finish. Additionally, we handle technical riders, ensuring all technical specifications are met, and coordinate directly with artists and event organizers to ensure seamless implementation of all requirements.
Fixed Sound System Installations
We analyze each space’s acoustic needs and design tailored sound system solutions by selecting and integrating the most suitable equipment from trusted brands, ensuring optimal performance.
Music Production & Post Production
Recording, Editing, Mixing & Mastering

Onda Mecânica offers services across all three stages of music production. We adapt resources to each project, including facilitating connections with recording studios that best suit the desired sound. Each service can be provided independently or as a complete package.
Creative Audio works & Sound Design
We create custom audio content for artistic installations, exhibitions, and other creative projects.
Room Acoustics
We offer Room Acoustic Characterization projects and acoustic treatment solutions for various spaces, as well as standardized acoustic assessments to optimize sound performance.

About Us

We are a specialized audio engineering company committed to delivering world-class sound solutions. With deep expertise in acoustics, technology, and artistic expression, we provide cutting-edge services in live sound, fixed installations, music production, sound design, and acoustic consultancy.

We collaborate with artists, venues, and event organizers to create immersive soundscapes that elevate performances and spaces, always prioritizing clarity, depth, and impact.

Every project is an opportunity to innovate and redefine the audio experience. Our focus on innovation, reliability, and long-term partnerships has established us as a trusted leader in the industry. As we expand our portfolio and develop proprietary initiatives, we remain dedicated to shaping the future of sound.
