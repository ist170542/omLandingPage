Onda Mecanica

Audio engineering solutions

Onda Mecânica specializes in audio solutions, including sound system design and implementation, music production, creative audio content, acoustic projects, and consulting services. We handle live sound, fixed installations, technical direction, and offer comprehensive music production services. Our expertise extends to creating custom audio content and providing tailored acoustic treatments. We are committed to delivering high-quality sound that aligns with the artistic vision of each project.

What We Do

1. Sound System Design & Implementation
• Live Sound – In collaboration with clients and partners, Onda Mecânica handles the entire system design, setup, calibration, and operation during events. Each stage can be carried out separately based on project needs, though we prioritize full-project execution.
• Fixed Sound System Installations – We analyze each space’s acoustic needs and design tailored sound system solutions by selecting and integrating the most suitable equipment from trusted brands, ensuring optimal performance.
• Technical Direction for Events & Venues – We oversee all sound-related aspects, including assembling specialized teams, optimizing sound systems, performing regular maintenance, and suggesting additional equipment. If a venue lacks the necessary equipment for an event, we leverage our extensive partner network to find the best rental solutions, handling logistics from start to finish. Our long-term goal is to build an in-house equipment inventory to meet these demands. Additionally, we manage technical riders and act as a bridge between artists and event organizers.
2. Music Production (Recording, Editing, Mixing & Mastering)
Onda Mecânica offers services across all three stages of music production. We adapt resources to each project, including facilitating connections with recording studios that best suit the desired sound. Each service can be provided independently or as a complete package.
3. Creative Audio Content (Sound Design)
We create custom audio content for artistic installations, exhibitions, and other creative projects.
4. Acoustic Projects & Implementation
We offer acoustic treatment solutions for various spaces and conduct standardized acoustic assessments to enhance sound performance.
5. Consulting Services
In all these areas, Onda Mecânica provides expert consultancy, with the option for third-party implementation of the proposed solutions.

Our Mission & Values

Onda Mecânica is committed to delivering top-quality services while balancing technical precision and creative expression. We believe that high-quality sound isn’t just about technical excellence—it must also align with the aesthetic and artistic vision of each project.
For example, in live sound projects, we ensure all technical requirements are met, while also selecting a team that understands the artistic style of the event. This synergy between technical expertise and artistic sensitivity enhances overall project quality and client satisfaction.
Building strong client relationships is a core value for Onda Mecânica. We see each project as an opportunity to establish long-term partnerships, fostering trust and enabling continuous improvement in our solutions. Our goal is to work with each client more than once, ensuring that every project benefits from an ongoing collaboration rather than being treated as an isolated transaction.
